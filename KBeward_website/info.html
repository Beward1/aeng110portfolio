For this project, we were tasked with selecting a communication technology and explaining how it works and its history using an infographic poster. I chose the landline telephone and researched it using different websites such explainthisstuff.com and howthisworks.com. I used Adobe InDesign to create the infographic and used various facts and images to portray the information.
